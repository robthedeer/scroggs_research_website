About Our Consultancy

With over a decade of excellence in academic research and writing, we've helped thousands achieve their educational goals

Global Academic Excellence Since 2015

Scroggs Research Consultancy (SRC) is a premier academic consultancy based in Zimbabwe, specializing in providing comprehensive research and writing solutions to students and professionals worldwide. With over a decade of experience, we've established ourselves as leaders in the academic support industry.

Our global clientele spans across six continents, with partnerships in prestigious institutions including MSU, UZ, BUSE, and Reformed Church University. We've successfully collaborated with clients from USA, China, Canada, South Africa, Zambia, Russia, Nigeria, and Botswana.

Our team of expert researchers, writers, and consultants are dedicated to helping you achieve your academic and professional goals through tailored services that meet international standards.
